About

I'm a freshman in the Jerome Fisher Program in Management and Technology at the University of Pennsylvania, studying for a dual degree in the Wharton School of Business and the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. The experience of living in a multitude of different countries and cultures has given me a multidimensional view of the world, which I continue to expand and explore every day. This website is a collection of the adventures and experiences I have taken to understand the world around me with multiple perspectives.
